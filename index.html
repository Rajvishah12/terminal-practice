cold beverages

Cold beverages are very refreshing on warm days.

In terms of cold beverages you can buy, starbucks has some higher quality drinks but Dunkin iced coffee is much cheaper. There isn't one that's necessarily better than the other, it really just depends on what you want at the moment.

Juices and smoothies are also commonly preferred choices on cold days. As they aren't caffinated and generally contain real fruit ingredients, many prefer juices and smoothies as healthier options.
